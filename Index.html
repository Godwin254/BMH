Insight

Better mental health is a web based online therapy platform that provides professional therapy services at affordable costs and in a convenient manner so that anyone who struggles with life's challenges can get help anytime and anywhere. This will be made possible by linking you with an online counsellor/therapist/psychiatrist who will in turn offer their professional services.We also have variety modes of communication which you will be able to select depending on your suitability as a way of communicating with your assigned therapist.These inlcude phonecalls, text messaging, virtual sessions etc.
